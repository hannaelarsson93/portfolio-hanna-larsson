Encouraging people to start podcasting

Acast always put creators first. Acast is a distribution platform for any kind of podcast shows. They want to have a community feeling and aid creators to focus on developing their shows, while Acast handles the monitization. Acast offer their clients three forms of subscriptions; Acast open, Acast pro and Acast enterprise. The mission for Acast is to be the #1 choice for creators and advertisers in Sweden.

Acast powers podcasters everywhere, helping them find their audience and make money from their craft, on their own terms. And this message needed to be communicated to everyone who every had a though of starting a podcast.

The Challenge

The brief

Hyper Island hosted a 48 hour sprint together with Acast. The breif for this sprint was to raise awarenss of Acast and find a way to make more people join the Acast network and start their podcasts at Acast. The main challenge was to create a campaign (preferably a 360 campaign) that clearly stated that Acast is the place to be if you have a voice and a story to tell. They wanted an innovative 360 degree campaign that was smart, funny and inspiring. It should make YOU all want to start a podcast today. With Acast. However, as Acast is still a start-up and the budget is limited. Therefore, the more organic spread the campaign could generate the better. Create awareness towards creators that are not part of Acast yet and communicate a cosistent message through all the marketing mix elements. Present the solution visually & verbally for Acast team memebers

The goal was to make it clear what Acast is and to drive creators to the platform.

I loved the challenge of only having 2 days to complete our idea and prototype. Working in a group of four to move along the process in the speed needed was a challenge.

Main insight

Barriers to pocasting

We chose to focus on the "Nextcasters". This is the target group that who want to start a podcast, but they haven't yet started one.

The campaign builds on the insight that a barrier to start a podcast is that you don’t know if anyone will actually listen to you. A theme was that people said “Does someone really want to listen to what I have to say?”. The idea of the campaign was build on this main insight.

Problem statement & How might we..

Defining the need

After we had understood what the main barrier to starting a podcas was we continued by formulating a "How might we..".

  • How might we get "newcasters" to feel like their story and voice matters and that there is an audience that want to listen to them?

Ideas

Ideation

We ideated and came up with different solutions to the brief. The two main ideas that we choose between to continue ideating where a 360 campaign with funny and relatable copy or a spone-word video ad.

In the end we choose to move forward with the idea of creating a 360 capaing with posters and simple video ads from the copy that I had come up with during the ideation phase.

Our idea

#Talk to those who listen

An integrated campaign with a fun undertone and relatable content for those who have a lot to say but no audience.

Marketing mix

We suggested Acast to run the campign in several different channels. We also gave them different options depening on how much money they were willing to spend. The channels that we could see the campaign run in were:

  • Printed ads around the city
  • Ads in sound format in Acast podcasts
  • Social media (Instagram, Facebook, Youtube, Twitter & TikTok)

Scale & Reachability

We suggested Acast 3 different options for channels depening on budget contraints. We advised Acast to go with alternative number 2 since this was almost the same cost as alternative 1 but would potentially reach a larger audience.

Alternative 1

Focus only on social media. With this alternative Acast could focus on oganic reach and only pay a minimun amount of money for paid ads in social media.

Alternative 2

Focus on social media and ads in podcasts. With this alternative Acast could reach an even larger target audience without spending that much more money since making podcast advertisements are quite cheap for them.

Alternative 3

With this alternative we suggested also including printed ads in the city. This would reach more people, but it would in turn also be much more expensive.

measuring success

Lead generation & conversions

It is always important to measure the success of a campaign when it is launhced. We suggested Aacast that they could measure leads and conversion from the campaign using a dedicated landing page using UTM links.

In the socail media ads there are clear buttons leading to the sign up page and in the printed ads there are QR-codes included so that the target group easily can scan the code and end up in the sign up page.
