About me

My name is Hanna Larsson and I am currently studying Digital Media Creative at Hyper Island. At Hyper Island I am learning the latest trends in design, technology, strategy and entrepreneurship, all with a focus on creativity and communication for the digital world. During my education I have led and participated in projects regarding UX Design and Development. In all projects I gained knowledge of user behavior and conducted user research together with my teammates.

I have previously completed a degree in international business and I also have a background from working as management consultant. As a consultant i worked both in project management and with change management. I loved the human interactions. However, I always felt drawn to tech, design and the amazing things that can be achieved with these. I am a firm beliver that design should be human-centered.

Because of this I started my journey to become a UX Designer in the fall of 2020. On this site I will share interesting projects and case studies of projects I have been working on. I can't wait to continue my journey and I hope that you want to follow along!

My values

  • Kindness & Positivity

    I am generally a very positive person. I like to spread my excess of positive and "can do" energy around.

  • Be humble

    Prestige is not really in my vocabulary. I take on every task with humbleness.

  • Love to solve problems

    As a designer you need to research and understand problems. You also need to love solving them.

  • Curiosity is key

    Being curious takes us a long way and it is a huge drive for me. My curiosity and eagerness to learn is for instance what drove me to code this website by myself from scratch.
